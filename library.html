Library | Computer Education

The school library is extremely rich with books in all the possible fields of education & information,other than the main stream books . It consist of more than 10000 books for all the subjects. Other than these there are many reference books available for science History & and also Biographies of great Personality of not only Indians but world renowned Thinkers, Philosophers, Statesmen, Scientists etc

The library hall can accommodate approx 100 students at a time . The selection of the books for the library is done keeping in mind that any school library is the foundation of a child's mental ability, future planning and also knowledge based society . The different levels of the mental development of an amateur mind are the main emphasis . The library is one of the favourite place for the students & staff.

Aaryan's school has the best infrastructure of IT in the city. The school offers computers Aided & IT education from class KG to X. computer aided education makes the learning interesting & effective . The IT education keeps the students at par with the latest technology. The students are encouraged to take part in the various inter school and all India level competitions presenting their works & projects.
